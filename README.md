**Gold Saving Scheme Analysis Dashboard | Power BI Project**

I am excited to share my latest **Data Analyst project** where I analyzed gold saving schemes offered by various companies using Microsoft Power BI 📊

**Project Overview:**

This dashboard provides insights into gold saving schemes, focusing on key factors like investment patterns, scheme duration, company participation, and digital adoption.

**Key Highlights from the Analysis:**

✔ Most schemes follow an **11-month duration model**

✔ **₹1000–₹3000** is the most common monthly investment range

✔ Increasing trend of **online gold saving schemes**

✔ High **customer ratings** indicate strong trust in these schemes

✔ Majority of companies are concentrated in specific regions, showing demand patterns

**Dashboard Features:**

• KPI metrics (Total Companies, Avg Investment, Most Common Duration)

• Scheme-wise and company-wise analysis

• Duration and investment distribution

• Online vs Offline comparison

• State-wise visualization using map

*Disclaimer:*

This project uses a *sample dataset created for learning and demonstration purposes* and does not represent the complete list of companies or schemes.

💡 **Tools Used:**

• Microsoft Power BI
